Features

📅
Flexible Posting

Post manually with one click, or queue series on a schedule and let the built-in scheduler handle the rest.

🤖
Human & AI Writing

Generate unique descriptions with different LLMs. Multiple variants per series — pick the best, or write and edit manually.

🖼️
Art Series, Organized

Organize artwork into named series. Track image status across the pipeline: pending → queued → posted. Drag-and-drop reordering.

📲
Multi-Platform

One post, several platforms — Telegram, Instagram, Facebook and Pinterest updated simultaneously. No copy-pasting, no switching tabs.
