Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

AI Art Publisher is a personal publishing tool used to manage and publish original AI-generated artwork to Pinterest and other social platforms.

Information Collected

The application may store limited account-related information required for publishing content through connected platform APIs.

  • OAuth access tokens
  • Platform account identifiers
  • Board or collection identifiers
  • Publishing metadata

How Information Is Used

Stored information is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating connected accounts
  • Publishing user-owned content
  • Managing boards, collections, and posts
  • Improving publishing workflows

Data Sharing

No personal data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Content Ownership

The application is intended for publishing content owned or created by the account holder.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored credentials and authentication tokens.

Third-Party Services

The application may interact with third-party APIs and services, including Pinterest, for content publishing functionality.
